Our Story

Helpa, Non-Profit Organization

It all started on a typical day, when we stumbled upon a staggering statistic. Morocco was grappling with a heart-wrenching reality - an estimated 3 million stray dogs roaming its streets, overlooked and abandoned. It was in this moment that the seed of our final year project was planted. Driven by a shared determination to make a difference, we embarked on a mission to be their voice. Thus, our project was born, fueled by compassion and a relentless commitment to alleviate the suffering of those who cannot speak for themselves.
